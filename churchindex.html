Why Us?

All our services are family-oriented, characterized by passionate praise and spirit-filled music, followed by a creative message that is sure to challenge and inspire you.In between our two main Sunday Services, we have Bible Study available for all groups so you can come to the service you are most comfortable with, and be sure to bring you're children along.
We invite you to make our Church your home.If you are a temporary resident of our area we encourage you to visit us.We are committed to watching over and caring for those who are members of a Church that is temporarily too far away for them to attend.
We encourage you to speak to one of our pastors about your decision so we may answer questions you may have about membership.The most important thing to us is that you have every opportunity to grow as a disciple of Jesus Christ.We believe that Church membership is an important element in this growth process.
We are a cross section of people from all over East Africa and beyond.You will find singles, couples, families - people who want to know God and build solid relationships.One thing's for sure, you'll fit in here!
