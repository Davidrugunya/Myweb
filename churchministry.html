OUR MINISTRY

Childrens' Ministry

Sunday School Schedule

8:30am - 10:30am
11:30am - 1:30pm

Young children are notably tender;their sincerity is never in doubt and their hearts are capable of genuine conversion.In fact,Jesus told adults that they must become like children to experience the new birth(Matthew 18:3).
The church that reaches out to its children has a better chance of reaching out to its adults.These children grow up to be adults who can nurture their own families to faith in Jesus Christ.
We should all remember that early conversion saves not only a soul but potentially points an entire life towards service to God and man.Be the example your child can copy.

"Let the little children come to me;do not stop them."
Matthew 18:1-4

Youth Ministry

Youth Service

8:30am - 10:30am
11:30am - 1:30pm

Our Youth ministry is about forming friendships,fellowships and spreading the Gospel.
We establish a forum for professionals of the Church to act as role models, give career guidance to the youth and school leavers, attachment opportunities in their firms and seminars on topical areas.
If you're looking for a place to meet friends, have fun and learn about God in the process, then our church is the place for you!

Matthew 18:1-4

Men's Ministry

Meetings

We help men develop a consistent walk with Christ throughout their lives.Teach leadership, development and responsibility and empower men to serve with excellence and integrity in whatever vocation they engage in.

We establish a business forum where people can be enriched with godly principles of business as well as building networks for the improvement of their businesses.
We also connect with the youth programs, participate in bringing up youth of integrity and create a social ground for men of our Church.

Women's Ministry

Meetings

We proclaim the Gospel to every woman, raising godly women with the purpose of impacting and influencing our communities.

We witness to the women, grow them into maturity, mentor them to acheive their God given potential, impact the world positively through the application of Biblical principles and help them cope with life challenges.

Couples Ministry

Meetings

A fellowship that meets the unique spiritual needs of newly married couples and create a forum for socializing and guidance from older couples.We help build strong Christ-centered families and to facilitate the growth in Christian maturity of young couples.
We help in growth in Spiritual Maturity through weekly Sunday bible studies, weekly prayer circles, monthly social fellowships and various ministerial activities.
We nurture strong families through mentorship programs and interactive Marriage Enrichment Seminars/Workshops covering topics such as Communication, Conflict Resolution, Handling Finances, Growing in Intimacy, Sexual Intimacy, Relationship Building and Accountability, In-laws, Expectations and Roles and Parenting Gods' Way.

Member Care

This department is responsible for the congregants holistic growth.Its responsibilities are:

Benevolence

This serves the membership in the event of bereavement,medical support and financial support to needy members.

Hospitality

It refreshes the membership with a cup of tea during Meetings and after service.Its key areas are:
1. Karibu team(Where we recieve our visitors)
2. Catering Team
3. Visitation Team.

House Group

This is the fellowship arm of the department.We encourage all our members to be part of a house group in their neighbourhood.
